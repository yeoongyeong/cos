  • How to measure body size
    BUST:
    Measure the most prominent part of your chest, with a tape measure over your shoulder blades.
    WAIST CIRCUMFERENC:
    Measure the thinnest part of your waist.
    HIP CIRCUMFERENCE:
    Measure the widest part of your hips.
    NECK CIRCUMFERENCE:
    Measure the bottom of your neck.
    SHOULDER WIDTH:
    Measure between the ends of both shoulders.
