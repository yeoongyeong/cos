PRODUCT CARE

Jewelry

HOW TO WASH AND CARE FOR YOUR JEWELRY
  • Avoid using makeup, perfume or hairspray when wearing jewelry.
  • Be careful not to come in contact with water.
  • Clean jewelry such as gold and silver with a soft brush moistened with lukewarm water. Scratching may occur if brushed with a rough brush. Dry the jewelry with a soft cloth.
  • Gently wipe the jewelry surface with a dedicated cloth. Be careful with tissue or paper towels as they may scratch the jewelry.
  • The use of bleach may cause the jewelery to peel off. Instead you can restore your jewel's luster by wiping it with a cloth slightly dampened with alcohol.
  • To prevent discoloration, store the product in a case that does not reach air well.
  • Do not store jewelry in a leather case. There is a risk of discoloration.
ADVANTAGES OF JEWELRY

Classic / Durable / Versatile / Daily Look Dress Up
