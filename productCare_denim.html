PRODUCT CARE

Denim

HOW TO WASH AND CARE FOR DENIM
  • Denim items do not need to be washed after every wear. After putting it on, walk it to let the air flow through.
  • For surface contamination, wipe only that area with a damp cloth or brush gently.
  • When washing denim, hand wash in cold water or use a mild washing course. Use mild detergent to preserve color.
  • It is recommended to wash inside out to retain color.
  • Tumble drying can reduce shrinkage, so hang to air dry. Denim's weight helps smooth out wrinkles. Avoid ironing as heat can weaken the fibers.
ADVANTAGES OF DENIM

Classic / Casual / Durable / Versatile / Suitable for everyday / wear

Denim is made from a denim material with a very strong twill weave construction. Denim is mostly made from cotton, which is 100% shaved and sometimes blended to give it elasticity. Originating from work clothes in the 17th century, today it is a classic yet durable garment that has become the basis for many styles.
