In December, the season to share happiness with people around you with a heartfelt gift has returned. It would be better if you could convey your feelings with a gift that you can keep for a long time.

From sustainable jewelery collections to knitwear crafted in a novel way, Junior Fashion Editor Meda Ntumba introduces a wide range of gift collections

FESTIVE KNITS, THE COS WAY New knitwear to add to the mood of the season Meet the COS knitwear with delicate colors and textures. The design was completed through a new 3D simulation production process, from soft wool material, to the modern-style Fair Isle pattern, and to sensuous details. As design assistant Alex Horvath puts it: 'Based on the swatches, sketches, and size information of COS, we created a 3D model with a team of experts to check the color and texture. It's a sustainable way to cut down on sample making. There are no logistics or shipping costs because the 3D team can send the design directly to the producer.' The COS design team went one step further and designed the product in an environmentally conscious way. We also slightly modified the existing stitches and developed a new Fair Isle pattern. TRACEABLE CASHMERE COS is designing products using materials that comply with The Good Cashmere Standard to ensure that cashmere products can be manufactured in a 100% sustainable manner by 2022. This helps us understand where cashmere comes from and protects the animal and producer communities. Francesca says: 'As cashmere products receive a lot of love, I want to design products in a more independent and sustainable way. It's like creating timeless products that will last a long time through an ethical manufacturing process.'
