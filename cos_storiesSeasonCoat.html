New Season Coat Introducing a variety of winter outerwear products, from teddy bear coats to down padding.
Meet the new season products with the COS design and styling team.

Coat season is back. A winter coat is an item that can be worn for a long time once purchased. Everyone has a good outerwear to wear throughout the winter season.

Many people are looking for the outerwear products they need for the cold and windy season. COS Design Team's Sarah Coates, Nicole Bischofer and Rebecca Liesenfield talked about must-have items from the Fall/Winter 2020 collection.

Teddy Bear Coat for Women 'The warm and soft teddy bear coat is great for creating a cozy look. It is a coat that can create a comfortable and natural silhouette with an oversized fit. Meet the teddy bear coat that will become a focal point in your winter wardrobe.' Styling Tip: 'One teddy bear coat can add a point to the overall look. Hybrid collar with detachable undercoat Pair it with this to complete an elegant look.'

Textured Coat for Men 'This is a coat made from a special fabric. Made in Italy with a soft texture with 100% wool and noil. Lined with recycled fabric made from recycled plastic bottles.' Styling Tip: 'Take an oversized fit coat with a cashmere scarf or hat for a warm and sensual look. Try matching the color products that will become a point together.'

Belted coat for women 'A classic coat with a modern style. Featuring a minimalist design and texture, it's functional with a storm flap and waist belt. Create a sensuous silhouette with a belted coat.' Styling Tip: ' Try it with knitwear in bright and light colors. Pair it with leather trousers or ankle boots for a sensual look.'

Tailored coat for men 'This is a timeless item with sensual details added to a minimalist design. The collar can be fixed with a hidden button, making it a good coat to wear even on windy days.'

Styling Tip: A tailored coat with a 'mélange texture' can be paired with bright colored knitwear for a soft look, or paired with a slightly darker colored knitwear to give a point to the overall look. .'

3-IN-1 Puffer Coat for Women 'This is a coat that can be worn in many ways. It can be made into a short coat by opening the zipper at the waist, and it is also a good product to wear lightly like a vest. The fit is adjustable with drawstrings on the inside, and the zipper doesn't show out for a clean look.' Styling Tip: 'For best use, wear it with a long cardigan . If you want to wear it like a short jacket, how about pairing it with jeans with a clean silhouette , an organic cotton shirt and a cashmere vest for a casual look?'

WATERPROOF RAINCOAT FOR MEN 'This is the first raincoat introduced by 'COS', a waterproof raincoat for men. it's designed to be water-tight and water-resistant.'

Styling Tip: 'A raincoat is a product that is a point on it's own. so pair it with a long sleeve T-shirt for a sporty look'm
